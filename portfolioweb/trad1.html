Spiraling Madness

Rig and Model

technical exploration of character rigging and clean topology. Original character modeled, designed, and rigged in blender. Used a combination of shape keys and bones to create a flexible rig that can be animated in a variety of ways. Described as athletic and uncanny, this character was designed to be a versatile asset to convey horror specifically.

Modeled and rigged in blender, with topology planned around the areas that carry the most animation — the torso, legs and hands.
